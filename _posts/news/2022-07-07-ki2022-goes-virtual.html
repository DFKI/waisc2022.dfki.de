We like to inform you that KI2022 conference in Trier, September 19-23, 2022 will be held as a fully digital conference due to the current dynamics in Covid-19 infections. We are in the process of moving the conference to a virtual format. More information will be available soon - stay tuned! We are looking forward to meeting you at the virtual KI2022 conference!
