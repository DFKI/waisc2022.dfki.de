--- category: news title: Submission Deadline Extended layout: news permalink: /news/2022-07-14-deadline-extension/ ---

The submission deadline for papers of the Workshop on AI & Digital Twins for Smart Cities has been extended by two weeks. Papers must now be submitted on July 28th. We look forward to your submissions.

Please note that the EasyChair conference management system (https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=ki2022) indicates the deadlines of the KI2022 main event (May 16 and 23). Submission for workshops is possible nevertheless. Please do so by selecting track W10: Workshop on AI & Digital Twins for Smart Cities.
